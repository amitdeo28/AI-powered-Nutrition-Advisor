Disease Descriptions

Diabetes: A chronic condition that affects how your body processes blood sugar (glucose).

High Blood Pressure: Sustained high pressure in arteries, increasing the risk of heart disease.

Heart Disease: Conditions affecting the heart, including coronary artery disease and heart failure.

Liver Disease: Disorders affecting liver function, such as hepatitis or fatty liver disease.

Stroke: Interruption of blood flow to the brain, causing brain damage.

Kidney Disease: A condition where kidneys fail to filter blood effectively.
